Amia Islamic Charity Foundation Uganda

Healthcare Initiatives

Providing access to quality healthcare services for vulnerable communities

Our Approach

We believe that access to quality healthcare is a fundamental human right.

  • Primary Healthcare: We provide primary healthcare services, including medical check-ups, vaccinations, and maternal healthcare, to vulnerable communities.
  • Secondary Healthcare: We provide secondary healthcare services, including hospital care, surgical services, and specialized care, to vulnerable communities.
  • Health Education and Promotion: We provide health education and promotion services, including health workshops, training sessions, and community outreach programs, to vulnerable communities.

Our Impact

Our healthcare initiatives have made a significant impact in the lives of thousands of individuals and families in Uganda.

  • Improved Health Outcomes: Our healthcare initiatives have resulted in improved health outcomes, including reduced morbidity and mortality rates, among vulnerable communities.
  • Increased Access to Healthcare: Our healthcare initiatives have increased access to healthcare services, including primary and secondary healthcare, among vulnerable communities.
  • Empowered Communities: Our healthcare initiatives have empowered communities to take control of their health, including through health education and promotion.
