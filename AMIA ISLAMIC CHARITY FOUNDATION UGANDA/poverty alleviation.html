Amia Islamic Charity Foundation Uganda

Poverty Alleviation Program

Empowering communities to break the cycle of poverty

Our Approach

We believe that poverty alleviation requires a holistic approach that addresses the root causes of poverty.

  • Economic Empowerment: We provide vocational training, microfinance, and entrepreneurship support to help individuals and families improve their economic well-being.
  • Education and Skills Development: We provide educational support, including scholarships, literacy programs, and skills training, to help individuals acquire the skills and knowledge needed to access better economic opportunities.
  • Health and Nutrition: We provide healthcare services, including medical check-ups, vaccinations, and nutrition programs, to help individuals and families maintain good health and well-being.

Our Impact

Our poverty alleviation program has made a significant impact in the lives of thousands of individuals and families in Uganda.

  • Improved Economic Well-being: Our program has helped over 5,000 individuals and families improve their economic well-being through vocational training, microfinance, and entrepreneurship support.
  • Increased Access to Education: Our program has provided educational support to over 2,000 children and youth, helping them acquire the skills and knowledge needed to access better economic opportunities.
  • Improved Health and Nutrition: Our program has provided healthcare services to over 3,000 individuals and families, helping them maintain good health and well-being.
