Why Donate to Amia Islamic Charity Foundation Uganda?

Amia Islamic Charity Foundation Uganda is a registered non-profit organization dedicated to providing support to vulnerable communities and individuals.

  • Zakat and Sadaqah: Your donation will be used to support our Zakat and Sadaqah programs, providing essential assistance to those in need.
  • Education and Healthcare: We provide educational and healthcare support to vulnerable communities, empowering them to break the cycle of poverty.
  • Emergency Relief: Our emergency relief programs provide critical support to individuals and communities affected by natural disasters and conflicts.

How to Donate

Donating to Amia Islamic Charity Foundation Uganda is easy and secure.

  • Online Donation: Click the "Donate Now" button to make a secure online donation using your credit or debit card.
  • Bank Transfer: Transfer funds directly to our bank account using the details provided below.
  • Mobile Money: Donate using mobile money services like MTN Mobile Money or Airtel Money.

Donation FAQs

Frequently asked questions about donating to Amia Islamic Charity Foundation Uganda.

  • Is my donation secure?: Yes, our online donation system is secure and uses industry-standard encryption to protect your personal and financial information.
  • Can I donate anonymously?: Yes, you can choose to make an anonymous donation.
  • How will my donation be used?: Your donation will be used to support our programs and services, including Zakat and Sadaqah, education and healthcare, and emergency relief.
